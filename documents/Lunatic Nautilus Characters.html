Dolph

The hero of the game. A former pirate captain, betrayed by his rival and left with nothing. Despite being a pirate and motivated by revenge, he’s a good man at heart.

Weapons: Axeguns, Swords, Rapiers, Axes, Pistols

Armor: Clothing, Light Armor

Command: Lethal Strike (Deal double damage to an enemy, but take recoil damage yourself)

Athena

A warrior priest, loyal to her king. She initially joins the party temporarily to help defeat an evil necromancer, but when the king is killed by said necromancer, she joins for good due to seeing the good in Dolph’s heart - and to honor her king’s last request. Pious and stern, she often comes into conflict with the other, looser members of Dolph’s crew.

Class: Paladin

Weapons: Staves

Armor: Clothing, Heavy Armor, Shields

Magic Set: Saint Magic (healing spells)

Innate: Cover (chance to take physical damage in place of an ally, based on Athena’s Defense and amount of damage ally has taken)

Medicine

Class: Lost Girl

Weapons: Maces, Staves

Armor: Clothing

Magic Set: River Magic (water/ice damage spells and time magic)

Command: Spirit Saw (manifest an ethereal chainsaw that does more damage to enemies with higher defense)

Sheva

A talking dog who fights the evil mutant Taco Tuesday in the ruins of an ancient civilization. She claims to be the last of her kind after the death of her parents and siblings. A standoffish loner at first, but this is due to her lack of experience with others, and she warms up over the course of the game. Fights with a cannon mounted on her back and knives used with her mouth.

Class: Commando

Weapons: Knives

Armor: Animal

Command: Shell (Use a consumable Shell or Grenade to inflict big damage and/or inflict status effects)

Command Set: Tricks (various buffs and debuffs)

Hope the Clown (real name: Sarah)

Class: Militant Clown

Weapons: Pistols, Rifles

Armor: Clothing, Tech

Command: Sights (Guarantee a critical hit next turn)

Command Set: Gags (various clown-themed skills that give indirect debuffs and status effects, as well as buffing the party)

Command: Concoct (combine two items for special effects, either positive or negative - you won't know what it does until you use it, however)

Sakasaki

A washed-up former samurai from the far East. The party finds him at a casino bar, and upon seeing the group and hearing their story, he decides to join them simply because they seem interesting. Loose and sarcastic, he carries a jug of alcohol with him wherever he goes and is almost always drunk - though this seems to help his fighting more than anything.

Class: Ronin

Weapons: Swords, Katanas

Armor: Clothing, Light Armor, Heavy Armor

Command: Kanenage (deal big damage to all enemies by consuming money)

Command: Ittenchiroku (roll a pair of dice for a random effect)
