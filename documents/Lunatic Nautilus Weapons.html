(WeaponAttack / 2 + 0..WeaponAttack / 8) * Dexterity - Defense / 2

The Knife is a small and fast weapon, allowing its user to dual-wield and strike multiple times. This makes it a perfect weapon for confident youngsters and energetic animals. (Pompilia, Sheva)

The Pistol is a simple gun that isn’t hard to use. It’s strong, and disregards enemy defense, but it doesn’t get stronger as its user gets stronger. It often sees use by more skilled fighters, as a side to their main specialty. (Dolph, Cleaver, Daraway, Hope)

The Rifle is a more complex gun, often carrying interesting properties, like rate of fire, crit rate, or even a special move. These are made for gun specialists, who have the accuracy and skill to use these the way they should be used. (Hope)

WeaponAttack + 0..WeaponAttack/4

Same formula as Pistol, the rifle’s benefits are in other areas.

The Staff is a long stick of some sort, sometimes used for basic striking properties, but often used to magnify magic power. Unless the user is trained, or the staff is specifically enhanced for physical attacks, using a staff in a physical manner often is quite weak compared to most weapons. (Calhoun, Daraway, Athena, Medi)

(WeaponAttack + 0..WeaponAttack/8) * (Strength + Intelligence) / 2 - Defense

Possibly a bit high concept, but the core idea here is that magic power helps even when physically attacking with a staff.

The Mace is a bludgeoning weapon, able to pound the user’s enemies to submission. They are known for their fearsome might, but can often be unwieldy at times. (Medi)

(WeaponAttack / 2 + 0..WeaponAttack) * Strength - Defense

More random than most weapons, but nowhere near as random as Axes.

The Whip is a weapon of control. It lacks in direct power, but can leave enemies Paralyzed. It’s the weapon of choice for badasses who want to leave a lasting impact. (Dwight, Daraway)

(WeaponAttack * 2 + 0..WeaponAttack / 8) * Strength - Defense * 2

Though the base damage is powerful, using whips actually increases the opponent’s effective defense, so it does much less damage to foes with stronger defenses. The paralysis can trigger regardless, however.

Claws are an unarmed strike for more naturally lethal animals. Its power might not be great on its own, but it can leave a lasting impact by making its opponents Bleed. (Yoan, Sheva)
