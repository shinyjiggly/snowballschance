General structure: Sequence of establishing shots of various areas while main theme plays, culminating in a logo animation. Then, a rapid-fire series of striking images from both cutscene and gameplay while Searching The Great Sea plays, cutting on the beat. Afterwards, as a stinger, a honk followed by a gunshot is heard, and a scene plays of Hope the Clown reloading a pistol and possibly saying something.

Establishing shots:

  • Daraway’s boat sailing slowly on the open sea, Dolph at the helm alongside Jeremiah. Daraway idly strums his instrument nearby.
  • A Natsiya fortress. Soldiers march in lockstep.
  • The ruins of the ancient society. Between destroyed buildings, barricades stand in front of parked bikes and wanderers relaxing for a moment.
  • An ominous tower of the necromancer’s palace, a blood red moon behind it.
  • A palace of the Far East, serene yet imposing
  • The planned city, beautiful and well-designed but strangely soulless

Striking images:

  • A massive battle between pirates and Natsiya soldiers, showing off the party size on both ends
  • A one on one duel between Cleaver and an enemy he’ll never actually fight ingame - in addition to showing off the flexibility of party sizes, this is a red herring that Cleaver will remain an ally
  • Medi rising out of a pool as Athena stands guardedly
  • Sheva atop a ruined skyscraper, howling mournfully
  • Sakasaki taking a huge swig from his alcohol jug, then slicing through an enemy soldier
  • The psychic kid blowing away party members with a burst of energy
  • Medi using her spirit saw against a heavily armored enemy, slaying it in one hit
  • Daraway using one of the high-level Cosmic spells to annihilate a group of enemies
  • Sakasaki rolling double sixes and annihilating a group of enemies with a suitably flashy effect
  • The party splitting in the evil castle, showing a bit of dungeon exploration and the concept of a variable party
  • Jeremiah leaping over a deep drop to a river at the uncharted island
  • Athena tanking a powerful-looking hit while the rest of the party is in low-health crouch, then casting a spell to recover everyone’s strength
  • A character dying, then auto-reviving through Feniks
