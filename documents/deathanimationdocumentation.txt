Death animation documentation

Frame| Description
    1| default
    2| flash disappear
    3| 15 degree turn back, bright red, colorize(hue 0,saturation 100,lightness 50)
    4| 30 degree (from default) turn back, medium red (h 0,s 100,l 0)
    5| 45 degree (from default) turn back, dark red, move 4 px down (h 0,s 100,l -50)
    6| erase every other line horizontal, move 8 px down
    7| erase every other line horizontal and vertical, 12 px down
    8| gone
